Our Services

1. Open Learning Programmes

Delivered at our dedicated office suites at Hateux Plateu in the heart of Musanze City, our open learning programmes and workshops provide capacity building solutions across twelve core faculties

Human Resource and Organizational Development
Leadership and Management
Accountability and Compliance
Public Sector Reform
Financial Management
Procurement
Monitoring and Evaluating Policies and Projects
Communication/ English Proficiency
Environmental and Natural Resources Management
Small and Medium Enterprises (SME) Development
ICT

These programmes are ideal for developing the skills and knowledge bases of key personnel from organizations. Through practical focus and interactive delivery, these learning solutions are designed to act as catalysts for change, improving service delivery and achieving their strategic objectives. Where relevant, these programmes also support private sector organizations to improve their commercial performance. In addition, we can deliver any of our open programmes as a group programme specifically for your organization’s staff. This can be delivered in our premises or in your organizations Our open learning programmes and workshops also provide an excellent platform to network and learn from peers from other organizations and countries who face similar challenges

2. Tailored Learning Solutions

We also recognize that sometimes organizations are presented with unique development needs that require a customized solution. For these situations we offer tailored learning solutions; these can be based upon our current portfolio of programmes and workshops or be developed at the specific request of individual organizations to meet their exact requirements. To ensure this is achieved we work in partnership with interested organizations from the outset- identifying their needs, key objectives and intended outcomes. We can also develop unique curricula and learning materials should they be deemed necessary.
